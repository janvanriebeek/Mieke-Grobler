My Fantastic Learning Programs!

Fine Motor Skill Development!(Grades 1–3)

These sessions focus on building the small muscle movements needed for writing, cutting, drawing, and everyday classroom tasks. Activities are fun, hands-on, and designed to improve hand-eye coordination, pencil grip, and overall control—key skills for confident early learning.

Mathematics (Grades 4–6)!

I offer guided support to help learners understand core math concepts like multiplication, division, fractions, problem-solving, and word sums. Lessons are broken down step by step to make math less overwhelming and more enjoyable, with lots of practice and real-world examples.

Mathematical Literacy (Grades 10–12)

These lessons help students understand how math applies to everyday life—budgets, interest rates, measurements, and more. I focus on practical problem-solving, clear explanations, and exam preparation to help learners succeed with confidence in this important subject.

Afrikaans and English Language Development (All Grades)

Whether your child needs help with reading, writing, grammar, spelling, or comprehension, I provide tailored support in both English and Afrikaans. Lessons are interactive, creative, and adapted to each learner’s level, helping them grow their vocabulary, improve sentence construction, and become confident communicators.

Exam Preparation and Study Support (All Grades)

I help learners prepare for tests and exams by reviewing key content, building study methods, and working through past papers. We focus on time management, reading questions carefully, and learning how to answer effectively under pressure.

Hi There! I'm Mieke, Your Learning Friend!

Hi, I'm Mieke Grobler, a passionate and dedicated teacher offering extra classes to learners from Grade 1 to Grade 12. My goal is to support each student’s unique learning journey by building strong foundations, improving confidence, and encouraging a love for learning.

I have extensive experience working with learners who have ADHD, dyslexia, and autism. I understand the importance of creating a calm, supportive, and flexible learning environment. I use individualized strategies—like multi-sensory tools, step-by-step guidance, and routine structure—to help every child succeed at their own pace. Whether your child needs support catching up, exam preparation, or ongoing enrichment, I’m here to help them reach their full potential.
